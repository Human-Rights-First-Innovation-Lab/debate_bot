"I was stunned to hear of the collapse of the I-5 Skagit River bridge, but I am thankful that no lives were lost.  This is a heavy-traffic corridor essential to trade and the movement of goods in our state.  I will be working with my colleagues to monitor the situation and ensure its quick resolution."
